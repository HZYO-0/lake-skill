# Evidence Ladder

Use this framework for every important claim.

## Claim levels

### Level 0: Fact
A direct statement or event from the chat record.

### Level 1: Pattern
A repeated behavior across multiple messages or sessions.

### Level 2: Psychological or communication hypothesis
A non-clinical interpretation of a repeated pattern.

### Level 3: Communication recommendation
A respectful next-step recommendation based on evidence and uncertainty.

### Level 4: Symbolic reflection
Astrology, tarot, metaphor, or literary reflection. Disabled by default and never part of the evidence score.

## Confidence rules

Low confidence:
- 1-2 pieces of evidence.
- Single incident.
- Low-quality ASR/OCR.
- Missing context.

Medium confidence:
- 3+ pieces of evidence.
- At least 2 sessions.
- Similar triggers.
- Alternative explanations reviewed.

High confidence:
- Repeated across time periods.
- Counterevidence reviewed.
- Not based on a single event.
- Still avoid certainty words.
