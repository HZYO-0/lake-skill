# Relationship Communication Patterns

Focus on interaction loops, not only the target person's traits.

## Positive loops

Examples:

- Self shares light daily detail → target responds → self does not pressure → target elaborates.
- Self acknowledges emotion → target softens → both repair.

## Negative loops

Examples:

- Self asks for relationship definition under pressure → target delays → self escalates → target withdraws further.
- Target gives ambiguous signal → self overexplains → target becomes quiet → self interprets silence as rejection.

## Conflict escalation signals

- Repeated interrogation.
- Sarcasm or indirect blame.
- Long emotional monologues after silence.
- Testing by coldness.
- Demanding immediate reassurance.

## Repair signals

- Apology with specific ownership.
- Reframing without blame.
- Giving space with a follow-up time.
- Naming needs clearly.
- Validating before asking.

## Communication recommendations

Always prefer:

- clarity
- respect
- consent
- boundaries
- low-pressure invitations
- specific repair attempts

Never recommend:

- jealousy induction
- silent treatment as a test
- emotional blackmail
- threat of withdrawal to control response
- repeated pursuit after clear rejection
