# Big Five Communication Signals

Use Big Five only as a non-clinical communication-signal framework.

Do not infer a complete personality profile from chat logs.

## Observable dimensions

### Emotional reactivity / stability signals
Look for repeated reactions to uncertainty, delay, conflict, ambiguity, or perceived distance.

### Directness of expression
Look for direct needs, indirect hints, jokes, evasions, silence, or meta-communication.

### Conscientiousness / reliability signals
Look for planning, follow-through, promise keeping, time arrangement, and consistency.

### Agreeableness / cooperation signals
Look for repair attempts, empathy, compromise, validation, defensiveness, blame, or contempt.

### Social energy / extraversion signals
Look for initiating topics, sharing daily life, sustaining conversation, inviting contact.

### Openness / exploration signals
Look for willingness to discuss values, future, novelty, inner world, or difficult topics.

## Forbidden overclaims

Do not write:

- 对方高神经质。
- 对方人格不稳定。
- 对方是某种人格障碍。
- 对方的真实人格就是……。

Use:

- 聊天中呈现……相关沟通信号。
- 该信号可能与……有关，也可能由……解释。
