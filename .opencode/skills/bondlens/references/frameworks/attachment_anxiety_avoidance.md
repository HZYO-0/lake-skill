# Attachment Anxiety and Avoidance Signals

Use adult attachment only as a non-clinical relationship communication lens.

Do not diagnose attachment disorder or assert a fixed attachment type.

## Attachment anxiety signals

Possible chat-observable signals:

- Repeated reassurance seeking.
- Strong distress after delayed replies.
- Fear that silence means rejection.
- Difficulty tolerating ambiguity.
- Urgent repair attempts after conflict.

## Attachment avoidance signals

Possible chat-observable signals:

- Withdrawing after intimate topics intensify.
- Delaying or avoiding conflict conversations.
- Emphasizing independence and space.
- Minimizing emotional topics.
- Changing topics when relationship definition appears.

## Secure functioning signals

- Direct but warm communication.
- Ability to express needs and boundaries.
- Repair after conflict.
- Consistency between words and actions.
- Willingness to negotiate closeness and space.

## Output policy

Prefer:

> 依恋焦虑相关信号中等。
> 依恋回避相关信号中低。
> 当前更像是“关系确认需求 + 高压力话题退缩”的混合沟通模式。

Avoid:

> 对方就是回避型。
> 对方是焦虑型人格。
> 对方童年有创伤。
