# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking corrective action.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant.
